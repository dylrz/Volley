Course Overview

- Get on the court, and stay close to volleyball

	- Come back from the summer with minimum rust.

	- Keep watching film and learn from high level volleyball

  
- Lift and stretch. Gain strength and mobility

	- Goal is to always be physically available next year.

	- Baseline is high, but being an athlete this is important.

  
- Eat a sustainable, nutrient dense diet

	- Find a diet that I can maintain through next year.

        - Keep track of macros/calories over the course of a week.

  
- Meditation/Focus

	- Perhaps more important than physical availability is mental availability.

	- Objective is to use meditation/focus to guide me toward more stability 
          in mindset, and thus my mood and motivation

	- Build habits that can be maintained throughout a year/season.

  
- Read books. Knowledge is power.

	- Read books that expand my perspective, and help build/adjust my mindset.

  
- Become less affected by externalities.

	- A question to answer this summer is how I can be less affected by my environment,
          shape my environment in a way that is conducive to my wellbeing, or both.
