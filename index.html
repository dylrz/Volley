Course Overview

Get on the court, stay close to volleyball

- Come back from the summer with minimum rust.
- Keep watching film and learning.

Lift and stretch, gain strength and mobility

- Goal is to always be physically available next year.
- Baseline is high, but being an athlete this is important.

Eat a sustainable, nutrient dense diet

- Find a diet that I can maintain through next year.
- Keep track of macros/calories

Meditation and Focus

- Perhaps more important than physical availability is mental availability.
- Objective is to use meditation & focus to guide me toward more stability in mindset and thus my mood/motivation.
- Build habits that can be maintained throughout the year.

Read books. Knowledge is power

- Read books that expand my perspective and help build/adjust my mindset.

Become less affected by externalities

- A question to answer this summer is how I can be less affected by my environment and shape my environment in a way that is conducive to my wellbeing.
